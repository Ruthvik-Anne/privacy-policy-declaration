TapCard

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: May 9, 2025

Overview

TapCard is a business card sharing app that lets you exchange contact information via NFC tap, QR code, and Bluetooth/Wi-Fi (Nearby Connections). This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what leaves your device.

TapCard does not collect analytics, serve ads, or share your data with any advertising network. There is no account registration required.

Data Stored on Your Device

All business card data is stored locally on your device in a private database:

  • Your own cards: name, title, company, phone numbers, email addresses, website, address, notes, and optional profile photo
  • Cards you receive via NFC, QR code, or Nearby Connections
  • Tags you assign to cards
  • A share history log (which card was shared, when, and by which method)

This data never leaves your device except as described in the sections below.

Permissions Used

  • Camera — used to scan QR codes and photograph physical business cards for OCR extraction. Camera images are processed on-device and are not stored or uploaded.
  • NFC — used to send and receive business cards via NFC tap.
  • Bluetooth & Wi-Fi — used exclusively for Nearby Connections (peer-to-peer card sharing). No data is routed through any external server.
  • Location (coarse & fine) — required by Android for Wi-Fi and Bluetooth scanning used by Nearby Connections. The app does not record, store, or transmit your location.

Google Gemini API

When you scan a physical business card, the app uses your device camera and on-device text recognition (ML Kit) to extract the visible text. That extracted text is then sent to Google's Gemini API to identify and structure the contact fields (name, company, phone, email, etc.).

Only the raw OCR text (no images, no device identifiers) is transmitted. This data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy and their Gemini API Terms of Service .

The extracted result is stored locally and used only to pre-fill the card editing form. You can review and edit it before saving.

Data Sharing

The only data that leaves your device is:

  • OCR text sent to Google Gemini when you scan a physical card (described above)
  • Card details you explicitly share with another person via NFC, QR, or Nearby

We do not sell, rent, or trade any user data to third parties.

Advertising & Tracking

TapCard contains no advertising SDKs and does not access your device's Advertising ID. There is no analytics, crash reporting, or behavioural tracking of any kind.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual cards and tags at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

TapCard is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
